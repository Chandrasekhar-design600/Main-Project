What is Polytechnic?

Polytechnic is a diploma or vocational course in which an institute focuses on delivering technical education("technical education is that, it makes the students skilled and technically fit for the industries. "). Generally, polytechnic courses are a three year programme after completion of 10th class. Polytechnic also helps students to start their career by getting a good job at a reputed company.

Benefits of polytechnic course

polytechnic would help you achieve practical knowledge of training on the basic level. After attaining a polytechnic diploma, you could straightaway apply for jobs in the same field and often in the government or private sectors.

Eligibility for Diploma in Engineering/ Polytechnic after 10th

As you want to know about the procedure of polytechnics, so here I am providing you the complete procedure for getting admission in Polytechnic course. Polytechnic is also known as Diploma course. For getting admission on the basis of 10th, you must have to clear JET (Joint Entrance Test) of your state. JET is a type of Diploma Entrance Test which will make you eligible for taking admission in any Diploma course. But, for appearing in the JET you must fulfill its eligibility conditions.

Eligibility Conditions for Joint Entrance Test:

>>>You must have completed your 10th from a recognized Education Board.

>>>You must have completed your 10th with English, Mathematics and Science as compulsory subjects.

On the basis of your JET rank, the better college you will get, as reputed Polytechnic Colleges have high cut-off ranks.
